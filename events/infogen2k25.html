INFOGEN 2K25
Join us for an exciting lineup of games and activities!
GENERAL RULES
  • One team per Department.
  • Only UG CS / IT / CA students can participate.
  • Registration fee is Rs 200/- per student.
  • One can participate in a maximum of two events, excluding online events.
  • Rules will not be changed under any circumstances.
  • Mobile phones are not allowed during the events.
  • Confirm your registration through this form on or before 10.09.2025.
  • Strict discipline should be maintained.
  • Formal dress code should be followed.
  • LCD projector will be provided for presentations.
  • Lunch, Tea, and Snacks will be provided.
  • A Bonafide Certificate and ID card are must.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • No TA/DA will be provided.
  • E-Certificate for all the participants will be provided.

SCRIPTA (Paper Presentation)

“Where Ideas Take Flight”


Topics:
  • The Role of IoT in Building Smart Cities
  • Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality in Education
  • Prompt Engineering: The New Skill for the AI Era
  • ChatGPT vs Traditional Search Engines
  • Blockchain Applications Beyond Cryptocurrency
  • Big Data Analytics: Turning Data into Decisions
  • Zero Trust Security Model in Cloud Computing
Rules:
  • Only Two Members per Team.
  • Presentation duration: 6 mins (4 mins presentation + 2 mins query).
  • Both members must be involved.
  • Soft copy of paper to infogencs25@gmail.com on or before 09.09.2025.
  • Maximum pages: 8; Font: Times New Roman, Size: 10, Line spacing: 1.5
  • Only PowerPoint presentations allowed.
  • Selected papers intimated on 10.09.2025 via email.
CIPHER CHASE (Clue Hunt)

“Crack the Code, Claim the Prize”


  • Two participants per team.
  • Sequence of questions; first-round answer is clue for next round.
  • Only one answer per question allowed.
  • Team with maximum clues wins; tie resolved by submission time.
  • Participants should not share clues or solutions.
GRAPHICA (Poster Making)

“Creativity Meets Clarity”


  • One member per team.
  • Theme given on the spot.
  • Posters must be prepared in MS Word during competition.
  • Pre-prepared posters or copied content not allowed.
  • Judged on creativity, clarity, relevance, and presentation.
  • Organizers may use posters for promotional purposes.
  • Time duration: 1 hour.
